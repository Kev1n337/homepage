  • Date: April 2016
  • Service: Web Development

Augmented Campus

I'm developing an augmented reality application in a team of four developers. The App helps new students at the THM to find locations and show them current events. Therefore, we implemented a 3D model of the campus and the possibility to scan room signs. We released the app for iOS and Android. The App uses Unity3D and the augmented reality framework Vuforia.

  • Client: THM MNI
  • Date: December 2016
  • Service: App Development
